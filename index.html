CCWS Banner

A lightweight, zero-dependency banner that shows Crisis Cold Weather Shelter status. Click the banner to see the modal/iframe behavior with automatic fallback.

Banner preview

Seasonal rules apply; if out of season you may not see the banner.

Configuration

To customize, place a ccws-banner.config.js on your host at one of: /ccws-banner.config.js, /config/ccws-banner.config.js, or /assets/config/ccws-banner.config.js. The banner prefers host config, then falls back to the repo default.
